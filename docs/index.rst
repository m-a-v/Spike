Welcome to the Spike App documentation!
=======================================

**Get The Most Out Of Your CGM Transmitter**
From alerting you of high and low glucose values to sharing them to a plethora of different platforms and allowing you to loop completely offline, Spike will change the way you manage your diabetes.
